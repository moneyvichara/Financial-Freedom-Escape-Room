🚀 Mission: Financial Freedom

Mission: Financial Freedom is an interactive, browser-based escape room game designed to teach core personal finance concepts in a fast-paced, engaging way.

Players have 15 minutes to solve at least 10 out of 15 puzzles to escape — while managing pressure from falling oxygen and rising temperature levels.

🎯 What You’ll Learn

The game covers key areas of personal finance:

🛡️ Emergency fund, health & term insurance
💸 Budgeting & cash flow management
📈 Investing (debt vs equity, passive vs active, direct vs regular)
⚖️ Portfolio strategy (diversification, rebalancing, goal-based investing)
📄 Documentation (nomination, will, financial access)
🎮 How It Works
Solve real-world financial scenarios
Make decisions under time pressure
Use hints and skips strategically
Reattempt incorrect or skipped questions
Escape by solving 10 puzzles before time runs out
✨ Features
Immersive escape-room experience
Dynamic urgency system (O₂ & temperature)
Instant learning feedback after each puzzle
Performance summary with insights
Downloadable completion certificate
🧠 Designed For
MBA students
Personal finance learners
Classroom simulations & workshops
Anyone looking to improve financial decision-making
